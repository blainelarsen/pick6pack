NFL Power Rankings 2022

1. Los Angeles Rams

This team hasn't run out of cap space yet. The Rams got significantly better with the addition of Bobby Wagner from Seattle and Allen Robinson from Chicago. With Jalen Ramsey, Aaron Donald, and Bobby Wagner this team is hungry to get back to their third Super Bowl in three years.

2. Cincinnati Bengals

Let's just say that the Bengals are going to be better than they were last year, which should frighten most of the league. The Bengals completely revamped their offensive line to protect their franchise QB Joe Burrow for many years to come. This team is young and is going to be dangerous for years to come.

3. Buffalo Bills

The Bills front office brought in Von Miller to help bolster their defense even more. Bills had the #1 ranked secondary last season all while losing their star corner Tre'Davious White later in the season. With Von Miller's leadership and experience he is going to give the secondary even more opportunities.

4. Green Bay Packers

The Packers are going to be as dangerous as ever even after a devastating playoff loss to the 49ers. The Packers front office had a strong draft and even while losing Davante Adams in the offseason they have proven a strong 7-0 record without him. Looking at their defense on paper you would expect them to have a top three defense in the league.
